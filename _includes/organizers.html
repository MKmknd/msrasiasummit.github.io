Yutaro Kashiwa is an assistant professor at Nara Institute of Science and Technology (NAIST), Japan. He worked for Hitachi Ltd. as a full-time software engineer for two years before spending three years as a research fellow of the Japan Society for the Promotion of Science. He received his Ph.D. degree in engineering from Wakayama University in 2020. His research interests include empirical software engineering, specifically the analysis of software bugs, testing, refactoring, and release.
